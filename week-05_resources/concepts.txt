#Concept 1 - Tokens -Tokens are the unit of everything. When you pay for an LLM API, you pay per token. When the model 'thinks,' it thinks one token at a time.

#Concept 2 - Context Window - If you want the model to remember something across many turns, re-state it. Don't trust short-term memory."


Concept 3 - next token prediction - The model is rolling a weighted dice at every token. Temperature controls how weird the dice is.

Concept 4 - Hallucination - not true, but model give  the answer confidently.

what;s the weather today



Three rules from today. Burn them in.
One: When you need facts, verify them yourself. Don't trust the model on dates, numbers, names, quotes, or anything specific.
Two: When you can — give the model the facts in the prompt. If you want it to summarise a news article, paste the article. The model can't hallucinate something that's already in its context.
Three: Confidence is not evidence. The model sounds confident about everything. Including the wrong things. Especially the wrong things. Your eyes are the only quality check.
