Prompt Engineering (specifications, not a wish)

Amateurs - help with an essay - wish

Pros - what, for whom, in what shape

Toolkit

1. Role - who it to be - You are a [tutor / editor / planner]..." (the system message)
2. Context - The user is a 15-year-old who..." - "Who's using your tool, and what do they already know?"
3. Specificity - say exactly what you want, not vaguely. "Summarize" → "Summarize in 3 bullet points a busy student could read in 10 seconds."
4. Output Format - dictate the shape. "Answer as a numbered list / a single sentence / JSON."

Few shot(prompt Engineering technique) - When you can't describe the format in words, just show it. Examples beat instructions.
